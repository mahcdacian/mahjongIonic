How to Play (English) 

1. Enter the game by enrolling and creating your account in the next page.

2. Login to your account on www.vitasoy80th.com and scan the QR code you've received with the purchase of any specially-packaged 6-pack Vita or Vitasoy drink.

3. You will receive a winning combination of mahjong tiles. Different sets of winning mahjong sets mean more points!

4. Check your total score and winning mahjong combos in the "My Score" page.

5. You can check "Top Players" to see how you rank against your region!

6. If you add the Mahjong web game as a shortcut on your Home Screen, you can receive an additional 1000 points.

Disclaimer: Entrants must be a resident of USA or Canada at the time of account registration.

遊戲玩法  

1. 在下一頁註册並建立帳戶來進入遊戲。

2. 登入www.vitasoy80th.com，掃描任何特殊包裝的維他或維他奶6包裝飲品上的二維碼。

3. 您將獲得一手麻將牌。更好的手牌意味著更多的積分！

4. 在“得分記錄”頁面查看您的總分數和贏得的麻將組合。

5. 你可以在“得分記錄”頁面中查看你的總分數。

6. 如果將維他奶麻將遊戲添加到桌面，則可以獲取額外1000點積分。

免責聲明：玩家必須在遊戲期間是美國或加拿大居住民才能領取獎品。
